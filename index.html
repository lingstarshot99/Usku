About Usku

USKU is aimed to help people connect with one another in a physical and personal sense and make life easier for everyone. USKU is a community that wants neighbours to know each other, for people to help one another and to save the earth from wastage.

USKU desires people to help one another instead of selling a service and we want you to be a part of this community too!
